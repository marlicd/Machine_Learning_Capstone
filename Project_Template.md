1. What is the problem you are solving?

Many people are at risk of stroke without knowing it. This project predicts whether a person is likely to have a stroke using their health and lifestyle information, helping with early detection.

2. What tools did you use?
Python
Pandas
NumPy
Matplotlib
Seaborn
Scikit-learn
Jupyter Notebook
Kaggle dataset
3. What insights did you or want to discover? / What solutions do you want to offer?

I want to find the factors that increase the risk of stroke and build a model that can predict stroke early. This can help healthcare providers identify high-risk patients before a stroke happens.

4. How would a business or community benefit from your work?

Hospitals and clinics can identify high-risk patients earlier and make better treatment decisions. This can improve patient care, reduce healthcare costs, and help prevent strokes through early intervention.
